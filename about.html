Welcome to Try-Catch {films}! This is an experimental film project where participants are randomly assigned a concept for a film to make. Once completed, the film will be featured on this website.

Below, you will find an example of the randomization engine. Play around with it. See what you think. If you would like to get a formal assignment for submission to our site, click the link above. Unlike this sample generator, you only get one chance to generate an official concept for your film. Once completed, upload your film to your own YouTube channel and use our film submission form to get your film live on our site!
